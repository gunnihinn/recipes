# Lagterta

## Cake

* 250 g soft butter
* 325 g sugar
* 500 g flour
* 2 large room temperature eggs
* 1 tsp baking soda
* 2 tsp vanilla
* 3 dl milk
* 3 tbsp cocoa
* 2 tbsp cloves
* 1 tbsp cinnamon

## Butter cream

* 500 g confectioner sugar
* 150 g soft butter
* 1 egg

Heat oven to 180Â°C convection.
Whip butter and sugar together.
Add one egg at a time.
Mix dries together.
Add half of dries and half of milk and vanilla at a time.
Spread dough on two baking plates.
Bake for 17-18 minutes.

Cut each plate of cake in two and join them with frosting, then frost the top of one and join the two.
Cut in two or three pieces for serving or freezing.
