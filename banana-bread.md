# Banana bread

Makes 12 small cakes or one big one.

* 2 cups (250g) all-purpose flour (spooned & leveled)
* 1 tsp baking soda
* 1/4 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp cinnamon
* 1/2 tsp cloves
* 112g room-temperate butter
* 150 gr brown sugar
* 2 large eggs, at room temperature
* 80 g plain Greek yogurt at room temperature
* 345 g mashed bananas (about 3–4 ripe bananas)
* 1 tsp pure vanilla extract

Heat oven to 180°C.
Whisk dries together.
Beat butter and brown sugar until light and creamy.
Add eggs one at a time.
Beat in yogurt, vanilla and mashed bananas.
Add dries to wets and combine.
Bake for 60 minutes if making one big cake; otherwise 30 minutes.


## Whipped vanilla ganache

* 200g pure white chocolate minimum 28% cocoa butter
* 350ml 35% heavy whipping cream, divided
* 1 vanilla bean or 2 tsp vanilla bean paste
* 1 tsp (5ml) pure vanilla extract

Heat 1/3 of cream until simmering and pour over chocolate to melt it.
Let stand for 1 minute, then whisk to combine.
Once cooled, add in rest of cream.
Cover and refrigerate for at least 6 hours, preferably overnight.
Whip to stiff peaks for piping.
