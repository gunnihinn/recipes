# Riz au lait

* 200 g rice
* 300 g milk
* vanilla
* 40 g sugar

Parboil the rice in water, then strain.
Add milk, vanilla and sugar.
Heat on low for 10 minutes.

## For a dessert

* 250 g cream

Cool the rice.
Whip the cream and fold it into the cool rice.

Serve with caramel sauce and sliced almonds.
